# Search Sources by Location

This reference helps the skill find activities efficiently. Over time, the skill
updates `memory/resources.json` with sources that proved useful, making future
searches faster and more targeted.

## Global Sources (work everywhere)

- **Eventbrite**: eventbrite.com/d/[country]/[city]/events/ — great for organized events
- **Facebook Events**: facebook.com/events/search — broad coverage, local events
- **Meetup**: meetup.com/find/ — community groups, hobbies, board games
- **TripAdvisor**: tripadvisor.com — activities, tours, things to do
- **Google Events**: search "[city] events [date]" — aggregates from many sources

## Italy

### General
- **RomaToday/MilanoToday/etc.**: [city]today.it/eventi — best local event aggregator
- **Zero.eu**: zero.eu/[city]/ — nightlife, music, culture
- **Eventbrite Italia**: eventbrite.it/d/italia/[city]/
- **Turismo Roma/Milano/etc.**: turismoroma.it, turismo.milano.it — official tourism

### Rome Specifically
- **RomaToday Eventi**: romatoday.it/eventi — the go-to for Rome events
- **Roma Culture**: culture.roma.it — official cultural events
- **Auditorium Parco della Musica**: auditorium.com — concerts, shows
- **Concertiaroma.com**: live music listings
- **CosaFareARoma.it**: guides by category (jazz clubs, outdoor, etc.)
- **Romeing.it**: English-language events guide
- **Zero Roma**: zero.eu/roma/ — nightlife and culture
- **Spin Time Labs**: spintimelabs.it — community events, San Giovanni area
- **Monk Roma**: monkroma.it — live music, craft beer
- **Elegance Cafe**: elegancecafe.it — jazz concerts
- **Big Mama**: bigmama.it — blues and live music (Trastevere)
- **Bebop Jazz Club**: jazz and blues (Testaccio)
- **Lanificio 159**: live music venue (Pietralata)
- **Giornate FAI**: fondoambiente.it — seasonal open heritage sites

### Milan

- **YesMilano**: yesmilano.it — official events

## Board Games / Social Gaming (International)
- **Meetup.com**: Search "board games [city]" — usually the best source
- **BoardGameGeek**: boardgamegeek.com/forum — community events
- **Facebook Groups**: Search "[city] board games" or "[city] giochi da tavolo"

## Music-Specific Sources
- **Songkick**: songkick.com — concerts worldwide
- **Bandsintown**: bandsintown.com — follow artists, discover local shows
- **Resident Advisor**: ra.co — electronic/club music
- **JazzNearYou**: jazznear.com — jazz events worldwide

## Free Events / Budget
- Search: "[city] eventi gratuiti [month] [year]"
- Search: "[city] free events this weekend"
- Search: "[city] cose da fare gratis"
- Many museums have free days (first Sunday of month in Italy)
- Parks, street markets, and festivals are usually free
